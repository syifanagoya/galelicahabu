Who Is Oriana Schneps From Below Deck Adventure

March 2024 · 4 minute read

The inaugural season of Bravo’s Below Deck franchise is titled Below Deck Adventure. It will feature nine new cast and crew members, all of whom will make their television debuts.

Mercury, the yacht captained by Captain Kerry Titheradge, has Oriana Schneps on board. In spite of her Bravo profile claiming that she had previously worked as a head stew, Oriana will join the internal crew of Below Deck Adventure as a stew.

According to her bio on the network’s website:

Oriana is a hard worker who has worked as a chief stew in the past and is eager to prove herself on the interior crew. She must show that she can rise above a battle to finish the assignment when she becomes embroiled in one within her department.

Since she started working at a Boston aquarium, Oriana Schneps has been captivated by the water. She found her calling in college after taking part in a scuba diving research mission in the Bahamas, according to her Bravo bio. She consequently started sailing soon after earning her degree from the University of Massachusetts Amherst.

According to Oriana’s profile on LinkedIn, she has a ton of experience. She started her work with International Yachting as a chief cook, where for almost two years she “managed international operations of vessels up to 200 feet.”

In 2018, Oriana started working as the estate operations manager at a family estate with multiple properties, where she stayed for just five months (June–October).

She started an online store called Doon Designs in 2019 while working full-time as an Account Executive at VaynerMedia, according to her LinkedIn profile. She changed jobs the same year, taking a position as an account executive with The Sasha Group, where she will stay through March 2020.

Oriana Schneps moved jobs once again during the epidemic and was employed by String and Key as a Senior Strategic Analyst. In August 2021, she gave notice in order to join the cast of Bravo’s Below Deck Adventure.

The businessman co-founded the cutting-edge blockchain company Lazy 8ight Boat Club this year and joined United Yacht Sales as a yacht broker. In addition, Oriana founded Disco House NYC, a digital media company that she will lead through July 2020.

Disco House NYC was founded in May 2018. Oriana also has a great Instagram profile where she documents all of her travels and frequently posts pictures with the man she is dating. John Dampeer, who runs the Fool’s Errand pub, is her love interest. Oriana has been hired for Bravo’s Below Deck Adventure in the meantime.

The inside team led by Captain Kerry Titheradge will include Oriana Schneps. She will split the role of stew with Kasie Faddah, and both of them will answer to Faye Clarke, the head stew. Along with the aforementioned cast, Below Deck Adventure season 1 will also feature cook Jessica Condy, bosun Lewin Lupton, and deckhands Nathan Morely, Michael Gilman, and Kyle Dickard.

The fourth spin-off series for the franchise will be this one. There are other variations of Below Deck, including Below Deck Mediterranean, Below Deck Sailing Yacht, and Below Deck Down Under. Below Deck is the original.

These programs each have their own captain and crew. On November 1, 2022, Below Deck Adventure 2022 will premiere its inaugural season. The show will have a new episode debut on Bravo every Tuesday at 9:00 p.m. ET.
