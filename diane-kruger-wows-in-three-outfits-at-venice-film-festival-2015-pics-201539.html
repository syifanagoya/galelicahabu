Diane Kruger Wows in Three Outfits at Venice Film Festival 2015: Pics

May 2024 · 1 minute read

Diane Kruger is the queen of the 2015 Venice Film Festival red carpet! She wowed in three separate looks, all in just one day on Wednesday, Sept. 2.

Find out who topped the Us Weekly 2015 Best Dressed List

For the opening ceremony glamorous red carpet, Kruger, 39, glowed in a white Prada dress with crystal embroidery, a matching cape, and side panel cutouts at the premiere of Everest. She accessorized the luxe gown with silver platform Prada heels, a Lee Savage clutch, and Kwiat baubles.

Switching from regal to showstopping for the 72nd Venice Film Festival opening dinner, the Chanel model opted for an eye-catching red cutout jumpsuit. She accessorized the bold look with black heels and the silver “Infinite Space” clutch by Lee Savage.
